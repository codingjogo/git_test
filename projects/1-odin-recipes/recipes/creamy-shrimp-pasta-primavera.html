Creamy Shrimp Pasta Primavera

This colorful pasta includes red bell pepper, mushrooms, and fresh basil. "You can swap out ingredients and change measurements to your liking," recipe creator Littlechef writes.

  • Creamy Shrimp Pasta Primavera
  • Linguini with Roasted Broccoli Pesto
  • Lemon Asparagus Pasta

Ingredients

  1. ½ (8 ounce) package spaghetti
  2. 3 tablespoons olive oil
  3. ¼ cup chopped onions
  4. 1 large clove garlic, minced
  5. ⅓ cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  6. ¼ cup chopped red bell pepper
  7. 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  8. 1 splash white wine (Optional)
  9. 1 pinch dried oregano (Optional)
  10. ½ cup heavy cream, or to taste
  11. 3 tablespoons butter
  12. 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour (Optional)
  13. 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  14. 1 tablespoon freshly grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste (Optional)
  15. 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil, or to taste (Optional)
